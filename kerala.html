KERALA

kerala also known as the gods own country lies in the south western part of INDIA

It's divided into 14 states capitaled by THIRUVANANTHAPURAM

Malayalam is the most widely spoken language and is also the official language of the state

Kerala is the 13th largest Indian state by population

Kerala is one of the prominent tourist destinations of India, with backwaters, hill stations, beaches, Ayurvedic tourism and tropical greenery as its major attractions
